OUR STORY

WELCOME TO ALEPH JUNIOR SCHOOL

At Aleph Junior School, we believe in nurturing young minds through a comprehensive bilingual curriculum that prepares students for global success. Our innovative approach combines traditional values with modern educational methods.

We provide a safe, inclusive environment where students develop critical thinking skills, cultural awareness, and a passion for lifelong learning. Our dedicated faculty ensures every child reaches their full potential.

  • Junior education in English and French
  • Experienced and certified teachers
  • Modern facilities and technology
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention

KINDERGARTEN

Our kindergarten program provides a nurturing environment where young learners develop fundamental skills through play-based learning, creative activities, and social interaction.

We focus on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional development while fostering curiosity and a love for learning.

  • Junior instruction
  • Social skills development
